Octopus

The octopus is a soft-bodied marine animal known for its intelligence, flexibility, and ability to camouflage. Octopuses are found in oceans worldwide and are part of the mollusk family.

Habitat

Octopuses live in a variety of marine environments including coral reefs, seafloors, and open ocean waters.

Diet

Their diet consists mainly of crabs, shrimp, fish, and other small marine animals.

Intelligence

Octopuses are considered one of the most intelligent invertebrates, capable of problem solving, tool use, and escape behaviors.
